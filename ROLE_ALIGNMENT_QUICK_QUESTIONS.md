# Role Alignment — Quick Questions (5 min)

Ask these 5 questions back-to-back to quickly diagnose: **Manager vs. Tier 2 Support vs. PS Engineering**

---

## Q1: "What energizes you most at work—and what drains you?"

**Manager signals**: Team development, mentorship, process improvement, seeing people grow  
**Tier 2 Support signals**: Deep technical problems, customer gratitude, expertise, problem-solving  
**PS Engineering signals**: Customer partnerships, implementation projects, delivery, hands-on with customers  

---

## Q2: "Tell me about your favorite project. What made it satisfying?"

**Manager signals**: "Founded support function from scratch" / "Scaled team from 1 to 5" / "Built SLA process that stuck"  
**Tier 2 Support signals**: "Took a 1,000-bug backlog and resolved 90%" / "Diagnosed a production issue no one else could"  
**PS Engineering signals**: "Delivered a complex customer implementation" / "Architected a custom integration" / "Customer went live on time"  

---

## Q3: "What's your ideal split—hands-on technical work vs. strategic/leadership work?"

**Manager**: 70% strategy, 30% hands-on  
**Tier 2 Support**: 85% hands-on, 15% strategy  
**PS Engineering**: 50-50 balance  

---

## Q4: "How do you feel about customer relationships? Deep and ongoing, or more strategic?"

**Manager signals**: "I like them, but prefer managing through my team" / "Strategic relationships only"  
**Tier 2 Support signals**: "Love deep relationships with power users but prefer async (tickets)" / "Being known as THE expert"  
**PS Engineering signals**: "Love customer relationships, especially in-person" / "Understanding their business is the best part" / "Hands-on during implementations"  

---

## Q5: "How do you measure success in a role?"

**Manager metrics**: Team satisfaction & retention, SLA compliance, CSAT, employee growth, issue recurrence drops  
**Tier 2 Support metrics**: Complex problems solved, customer satisfaction, reputation for expertise, first-contact resolution  
**PS Engineering metrics**: Customer projects delivered on-time, customer success, repeat business, revenue impact  

---

## Scoring

Count the signals:

| # of Manager signals | # of Tier 2 signals | # of PS signals | **Best fit** |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4–5 | 0–1 | 0 | **MANAGER** ✅ |
| 0–1 | 4–5 | 0–1 | **TIER 2 SUPPORT** ✅ |
| 1–2 | 1–2 | 4–5 | **PS ENGINEERING** ✅ |
